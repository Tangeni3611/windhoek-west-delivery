Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?
A: You can place an order by calling us at (650) 681-9470 or by filling out our online order form.

Q: What are the delivery charges?
A: We charge a flat rate of $8 per delivery, regardless of the order size.

Q: How can I pay for my order?
A: You can pay by credit card upon delivery.

Q: What is the delivery area?
A: We currently deliver within a 10-mile radius of our partnered restaurants.
